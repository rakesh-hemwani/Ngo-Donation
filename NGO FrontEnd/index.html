Learn About Us

We Provide Reliable And Effective Services

NGO PORTAL is a leading child-centric humanitarian organisation.We serve every child who needs help regardless of religion, race, ethnicity, or gender demonstrating God’s unconditional love for all people. We are dedicated to improve the future of the most vulnerable children in India. We do so by joining hands with communities at the grassroots level, donors in India and across the world, civil society, corporates, and the state and central governments. Our aim is to create a world where every child is educated, healthy, protected, and respected.

Our Service Areas

Our focus is on helping the most vulnerable children overcome poverty and experience fullness of life. We help children of all backgrounds, even in the most dangerous places, inspired by our Christian faith. Together we can protect children today, and empower them for tomorrow.
